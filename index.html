Privacy Policy (Simple Meditation)

Last updated: June 23, 2026

"Simple Meditation" (the "App") respects your privacy. This policy explains how the App handles your information.

1. Information We Collect

The App itself does not collect any personal information.

All meditation session data (duration, count, etc.) recorded by the App is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to any external server. No account registration or login is required.

Information that the Apple App Store / Google Play independently collects (such as install statistics) is outside the App's control and not covered by this policy.

2. Network Communication & Third Parties

The App does not connect to the internet. It does not send crash reports or diagnostic data, nor does it include advertising, analytics tracking, or any data sharing with third parties.

3. Data Deletion

Uninstalling the App permanently deletes all data stored on your device.

4. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
